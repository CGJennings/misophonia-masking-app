Misophonia Mask

This app uses noise to help mask out other sound. It is meant for people with misophonia, but can be used by anyone who needs to focus or relax in a noisy environment.

Headphones or ear buds will work better than using a regular speaker. For best results, use headphones with active noise cancelling technology.

Different button colours produce different kinds of noise. Each colour masks certain frequencies (pitches) more strongly than others. Some colours may work better in certain situations or for certain people, but a good place to start is with red (3), pink (4), or green (5).

If your browser supports installable Web apps (Safari doesn’t), you can install this page as an app on your device. As an app, it works even if you are offline.
